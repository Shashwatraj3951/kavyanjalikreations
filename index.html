Kavyanjali is a 9-year-old crochet and paper quilling prodigy from India. She began crocheting in the year 2020, when she was just 7 years old and was living in Milton Keynes, England. Later, she developed an interest in Paper Quilling too. She became keen on arts and crafts during lockdown has become an impressive expert who creates beautiful crochet and paper quilling crafts.
